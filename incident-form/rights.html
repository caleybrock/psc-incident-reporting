Ensuring a stable testing environment

As part of our commitment to ensure an accessible and stable testing environment, candidates are entitled to a distraction-free and comfortable assessment.

If you believe your assessment has not met the Public Service Commission's Standards for Testing, or that you are being assessed in a disruptive environment, you may submit a report.

Candidates may self-report if an incident occurs during a test being administered by the Public Service Commission. Reports must be submitted as soon as possible, no later than 48 hours after a test is completed. By submitted an Incident Report, your assessment will not be scored until the report is resolved, and may be subject to a retest period.
